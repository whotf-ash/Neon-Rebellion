The Neon Rebellion

A Dystopian World, A Future Revolution

The Story So Far

In the year 2077, the world has become a neon-lit dystopia, ruled by a mega-corporation known as X-Corp. The streets of the city are flooded with cybernetic-enhanced enforcers, and freedom is a distant memory. But amidst the chaos, a rebellion rises. Led by a group of outcasts, hackers, and warriors, the Neon Rebellion seeks to take back the city from the grip of X-Corp and return control to the people.

With the help of the rebels, you will uncover the secrets behind X-Corp's control, navigate through the corrupt alleys of the metropolis, and ignite the spark of a revolution that will change the future forever.

As the rebellion grows, so does the danger. Your choices will determine the fate of the city and the future of humanity. Will you rise to the challenge and lead the revolution?
